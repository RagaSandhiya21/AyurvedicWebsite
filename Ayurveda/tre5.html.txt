<h1>Eczema (atopic dermatitis)</h1>
<h2>Treatment for eczema</h2>
<p>Treatment options for eczema include:
    <ul>
        <li>anti-inflammatory ointments-either topical corticosteroids or non-steroidal anti-inflammatory
            ointments such as pimecrolimus</li>
        <li>ultraviolet radiation therapy (phototherapy)</li>
        <li>coal tar-to reduce the itch</li>
        <li>oral medication (tablets).</li>
        
    </ul>
</p>
<h2>Symptoms of eczema</h2>
<p>The physical effects of eczema can include:
    <ul>
    <li>skin dryness</li>
   <li>red and scaly areas on the front of the elbows and the back of the knees</li>
    <li>watery fluid weeping from affected skin</li>
    <li>itchiness</li>
    <li>lesions (sores) that may become infected by bacteria or viruses.</li>
</ul>
</p>
